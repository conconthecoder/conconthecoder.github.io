The building blocks to the #1 Nation #wnation

Historical outcomes

  1. Describe the history and characteristics of the Chesapeake and New England colonies.
  2. Describe the history and characteristics of the Middle Colonies and the Carolinas.
  3. Describe the history and characteristics of the Caribbean, colonial borderlands, and Georgia.
  4. Analyze the main features of British colonial governments and economies.
  5. Analyze the main groups and features in British colonial societies in the late colonial period.
  6. Specify how the Great Awakening and Enlightenment affected the colonies.
