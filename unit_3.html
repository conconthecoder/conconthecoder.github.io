Historical outcomes

  1. Describe the causes, main events, and consequences of the French and Indian War.
  2. Explain how colonial responses to British attempts to assert greater control over the colonies after the French and Indian War led to protests and revolution.
  3. Understand historical sources, analyze them for point of view and credibility, and apply these abilities to AP multiple-choice questions.
  4. Explain how and why colonial protests transformed into a war for independence and describe what the Declaration of Independence says.
  5. Analyze how the Patriots won the American Revolution.
  6. Describe how various American groups affected the Revolution and how it affected them.
