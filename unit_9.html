Unit 9: What time is it?

What time is it?

Historical outcomes

  1. Describe American “Manifest Destiny” expansion beyond the Mississippi in the 1830s and 1840s.
  2. Explain what caused the Mexican-American War, the main actions and attitudes during the war, and the key consequences that came out of it.
  3. Explain how western expansion caused sectional tensions and how those tensions were resolved in the Compromise of 1850.
  4. Connect how events through 1856 increased sectional tensions.
  5. Describe how events from 1857-1860 increased sectional tensions even more.
  6. Write a well-organized, persuasive essay on historical subjects.

Notes

  1. Manifest destiny - the desire to move westward - was done by individiuals who wanted either to mine or to farm
    Texas was annexed and became independent in 1836; America did not make Texas a state to avoid growing conflict with slave and free states (it would make it unbalanced)
  2. The cause of the Mexican American War was that America wanted more territory, so they claimed California, Texas, and New Mexico
    Also claimed Orgen from the British
  3. Popular sovereignty - the concept that states would decide whether they would become free/slave states
    Clay's compromise of 1850 - slave trade was banned but slavery was still allowed, California was admitted a free state, and creating unorganized Mexico territory
  4. The Whig party fell out, and was replaced by the Republican party.
    The Bleeding Kansas was the result of increasing tensions over slavery, what convinced the Northerners to support the Republican party
  5. Dred Scott v Sanford - L for antislavery, because slave Dred Scott couldn't get freedom because he was considered property
    Kansas becomes a free state - the government said it shouldn't, but all of the people didn't want it so they win
    Abraham Lincoln starts to get into debate, winning over people with his moral views, ends up winning presidency.
  6. LEQ (Long essay question) - Make sure to include adequate evidence, conclusion is not neccessary but it helps
