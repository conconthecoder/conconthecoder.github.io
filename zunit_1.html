Unit 1 : Industrialization

The cryptocurrency (or crack) of the 19th century

Historical outcomes

  1. Explain the causes of the growth of industry in the late 1800s.
  2. Survey various attitudes toward wealth and its inequalities during Industrial Age.
  3. Describe what labor conditions were like in industrial America, how workers tried to organize, and the results of these efforts.
  4. Describe the main characteristics and changes of American cities in the late 1800s.
  5. Describe the new developments in consumerism and leisure.
  6. Trace the important developments in the arts and education from 1870-1920.
