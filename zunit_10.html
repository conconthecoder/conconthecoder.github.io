Historical outcomes

  1. Describe elements of the youth culture and why they appeared in the 1960s and 1970s.
  2. Explain how many American groups asserted themselves and their rights in the 1970s.
  3. Trace the strengthening of the modern environmental movement.
  4. Describe how Nixon escalated and then exited the Vietnam War as well as the other major aspects of his foreign policy.
  5. Describe the main domestic policies and events in the Nixon administration.
  6. Explain the events and importance of the Watergate scandal.
