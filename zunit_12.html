Unit 12: America and its future

Yay you made it to the end

Historical outcomes

  1. Explain the aspirations and accomplishments of the Clinton, second Bush, and Obama presidencies and the influence of increasing partisanship.
  2. Identify key new technologies that were developed moving into the 21st century and describe their consequences on the U.S. economy.
  3. Describe how the American people and their causes have changed in recent decades.
  4. Evaluate recent American actions in foreign affairs and the position of the United States in the world.

Notes

  1. Parties have become more distant and therefore partisan - people care more about what their party thinks that doing a custom build religion
  2. Big tech - internet was created, which means that beautiful and impactful technologies can be created like react js to save the world, but that also means that TERRIBLE ABOMINATIONS like the rigid and convoluted angular Js can be created
  3. Care more about the environment and global warming, but also care about topics like abortion, gay rights, and racial equality
  4. America remains one of the top powers in the world (competiting only with China), but still have to deal with wars in the middle east
