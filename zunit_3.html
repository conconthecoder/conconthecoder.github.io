Unit 3: Big boy war p.1

A fourth of world history in one Apush Chapter

Historical outcomes

  1. Explain why the United States became more imperialistic and the causes, actions, and consequences of the Spanish-American War.
  2. Describe American foreign policy in the McKinley presidency after the Spanish-American War.
  3. Explain the foreign policies of Roosevelt, Taft, and Wilson and how they worked in practice.
  4. Describe how World War I broke out and why the United States eventually got involved.
  5. Evaluate the contributions of the United States to World War I and how the country made those contributions.
  6. Describe the consequences of World War I in the world and in the United States.
