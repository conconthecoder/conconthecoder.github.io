Unit 1: Columbi

A fourth of world history in one Apush Chapter

Historical outcomes

  1. Profile the economy of the 1920s.
  2. Describe how the culture of the economy had changed from previous ages.
  3. Explain manifestations of conservatism in the 1920s.
  4. Outline the main policies of the Republican presidents of the 1920s.
  5. Explain the causes of the Great Depression and the severity of the effects on the economy.
  6. Describe what conditions were like for many Americans during the Great Depression.
