Historical outcomes

  1. Describe the significant economic and scientific developments in the 1950s.
  2. Survey the elements of American prosperity and youth culture in the 1950s.
  3. Describe those left behind by the prosperity of the Fifties.
  4. Analyze the strategy, events, and causes of the early Civil Rights Movement.
  5. Explain Eisenhower’s domestic goals and results.
  6. Describe Eisenhower’s actions in the Cold War.
